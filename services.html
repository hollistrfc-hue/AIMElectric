Our Services

Electrical Solutions

Residential, commercial, new construction, or repairs — AIM Electric has the skills and licensing to get it done right.

What We Offer

Full-Service Electrical
Contracting

From a single outlet repair to complete new construction wiring, we bring the same licensed expertise and attention to detail to every project.

Residential Wiring

New construction wiring, home additions, remodels, outlet and switch installations, ceiling fan wiring, and dedicated circuits for appliances. We work with your builder or contractor and make sure every circuit is safe and code-compliant before the walls close up.

Commercial Electrical

Office buildouts, retail spaces, restaurants, light industrial, and tenant improvements. We work from blueprints and coordinate with general contractors to keep your project on schedule. Licensed for commercial work in Oklahoma.

Panel Upgrades & Service Changes

Upgrading from 100A to 200A service, replacing old Federal Pacific or Zinsco breaker panels, or adding a sub-panel for a garage or shop. We handle the utility coordination, permits, and inspection so you don't have to.

Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, under-cabinet lighting, landscape and outdoor security lighting, parking lot lights, and decorative fixture installation. We help you plan the layout to get the right look and coverage for your space.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 (240V) home EV charger installation for all major vehicle brands. We assess your panel capacity, run a dedicated circuit, install a weather-rated outlet or hardwired charger, and pull all required permits.

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Tripping breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights, burning smells, aluminum wiring concerns. We diagnose the root cause and fix it properly — not just the symptom. Safety inspections available for older homes.
