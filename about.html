Our Story

Built on a Foundation
of Honest Work

AIM Electric LLC was founded with a straightforward goal: bring a higher standard of workmanship and customer service to electrical contracting in central Oklahoma. Too many homeowners and business owners had been burned by shoddy work, surprise invoices, and contractors who disappear after the check clears.

We built AIM around the opposite approach — clear communication before, during, and after every job, transparent pricing, and work that holds up to inspection and to the test of time.

Based in Stillwater, we serve the surrounding communities including Perkins, Cushing, Guthrie, Edmond, and beyond. Residential or commercial, small repair or large new construction — we bring the same level of professionalism to every project.

Credentials

Licensed, Insured, and
Ready to Work

AIM Electric LLC holds all required state and local licenses for electrical contracting in Oklahoma. We carry full liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage on every job — protecting you, your property, and our crew.

Before any work begins, we pull the appropriate permits and ensure every installation meets the National Electrical Code (NEC) and local amendments. When the work is done, it passes inspection — every time.
